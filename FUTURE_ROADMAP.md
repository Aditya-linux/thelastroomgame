# 🚀 Future Roadmap: The Last Room

This document outlines the strategic vision and upcoming features for **The Last Room** as it evolves into a high-scale competitive puzzle arena.

## 🃏 1. Content Expansion: The 56 Card Deck
We will implement 56 unique puzzle rooms, each corresponding to a specific playing card in a full deck (including Jokers).
- **Ascending Difficulty**: As the card value increases (2 → 10 → J → Q → K → A), the complexity of the ciphers, grids, and logic gates will scale aggressively.
- **Suit Specialties**: Each suit will focus on a specific cognitive skill:
  - **Hearts**: Visual and Spatial puzzles.
  - **Clubs**: Pattern recognition and Logic.
  - **Diamonds**: Advanced Cryptography and Ciphers.
  - **Spades**: Meta-puzzles and OSINT (research-based).

## ⚡ 2. Infrastructure & Scalability
To support a massive player base, the backend will be optimized for performance:
- **Concurrent Users**: Target support for **1,000 to 5,000+** simultaneous users per lobby.
- **Latency**: Sub-100ms response times for answer submissions and hint broadcasts.
- **Edge Computing**: Utilizing Vercel Edge Functions to handle traffic spikes globally.

## 🏆 3. NFT-Style Shareable Achievements
We will implement a visual "Share Card" system for users to showcase their progress:
- **Generation**: Automated generation of personalized NFT-style cards (e.g., SVG/PNG) showing the player's Rank, Difficulty Cleared, and Suit Level.
- **Social Proof**: One-click sharing to X (Twitter) and WhatsApp to drive community growth.

## 📊 4. Universal Leaderboards
A centralized ranking system to track the elite players:
- **Global Rank**: Based on total games won and time-to-solve metrics.
- **Suit Rankings**: Specialized leaderboards for the "Kings" and "Queens" of each suit.
- **Live Tracking**: Real-time display of all joined users from #1 to the last entry.

## 💰 5. Open-Cap Prize Pools
Transitioning to a high-stakes economy:
- **Scalable Pools**: Example entry at **₹100** per user with **no participant limit**.
- **Dynamic Growth**: The more players join, the higher the bounty grows in real-time.
- **Winners Circle**: Multi-winner payouts for top 1%, 5%, and 10% finishes.

## ⏱️ 6. Elimination Mechanics
Adding pressure and risk to the arena:
- **Time Limits**: Hard limits of **2 to 5 minutes** per puzzle.
- **Instant Elimination**: Failure to solve within the countdown results in immediate disqualification from the room.
- **Live Death-Watch**: Visual indicators of how many players are being "eliminated" in real-time during a lobby.
